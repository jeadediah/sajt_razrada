FLOOR PLAN DESIGN

After the measures taken and the recorded condition, the possibilities of the subject space are examined. The space is examined through zoning and organization proposals in several variants, until the desired one is reached that will harmonize the client's needs with the possibilities of the space. In this phase, the purpose and dimensions of the room, positions and dimensions of furniture, doors and windows, as well as the positions of sanitary and heating bodies, refrigeration devices are defined. After the final variant of the basic function is approved by the client, further elaboration of the appearance of individual positions, materialization, position of sockets and switches, suspended ceilings as well as ceiling lighting, elaboration of details and others included in the project and the offer.

INTERIOR DESIGN

Based on consultations and reference examples from practice, the direction and style of the interior is defined. In accordance with the wishes of the client and the approximate budget, further elaboration of the interior is entered - combinations of materials, composition of elements, definition of accents in the space and more. Ideas are developed and visualized in the form of 3D views of the rooms covered by the project. During the creation of 3d views, topics such as the position of sockets and switches, the connection of switches and lighting are opened, which introduce the preparation of the preparation of documentation for execution, and after the approved 3d views by the client.

FURNITURE DESIGN

Design and elaboration of external appearance as well as internal organization of custom carpentry elements. Defining materials, handles, mechanisms, integrated lighting and other intended elements. Preparation of graphic (drawings of individual positions) and textual documentation (inquiries) for the carpenter on the basis of which precise and comparable offers for making and assembling positions can be obtained.

EXECUTIVE DESIGN

Preparation of graphic documentation required for the execution of works. Documentation is prepared on the basis of consultations, approved basis of function and 3d display. The documentation is divided according to the groups of works - demolition, masonry, ceiling base with lighting, base of sanitary, heating bodies, refrigeration devices, base of sockets and switches, sound system and all works envisaged by the project. It includes the production of schemes - facade and interior carpentry, aluminum, locksmithing, elaboration of details for execution. Together with the bill of quantities, it is an integral part of the documentation on the basis of which precise and comparable bids for the realization of works can be collected.

QUANTITY ESTIMATE

It represents the specification and calculation of the required works. Together with the graphic, it is an integral part of the documentation for the execution, on the basis of which precise and comparable bids for the realization of works can be collected.
